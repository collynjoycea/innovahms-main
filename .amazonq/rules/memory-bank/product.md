# Innova HMS - Product Overview

## Project Purpose
Innova HMS (Hotel Management System) is a full-stack web application for managing hotel operations. It serves multiple hotel properties (Innova Suites, Vision Suites) and provides role-based dashboards for every stakeholder in a hotel business.

## Value Proposition
- Unified platform for hotel owners, staff, and guests
- AI-powered concierge and room recommendations
- Integrated online payment via PayMongo (GCash, Maya, QR Ph, Card)
- Real-time room status, housekeeping, and inventory tracking
- Loyalty/rewards program for guests

## Key Features & Capabilities

### Guest / Customer
- Browse hotels, rooms, facilities, and features
- Book rooms with online payment (PayMongo integration)
- Virtual 360° room tours (Marzipano)
- AI concierge assistant (Rasa chatbot)
- Neighborhood map (Leaflet/OpenStreetMap)
- Loyalty rewards program
- Google & Facebook OAuth login

### Owner Dashboard
- Room management (CRUD, images, pricing)
- Reservation management
- Customer management
- Housekeeping oversight
- Inventory overview
- Staff management
- Reports & reviews

### Front Desk Staff
- Reservation management (create, view, check-in, check-out, extend)
- Guest profiles & loyalty points
- Room map assignment
- Shift profile management

### HR & Payroll
- Employee directory
- Attendance tracking
- Payroll processing & payslips
- Performance reviews
- Employment contracts
- Workload & task completion tracking
- Staffing estimator
- Shift scheduling

### Housekeeping & Maintenance
- Task assignment and tracking
- Room status map
- Linen inventory
- Maintenance reports
- Housekeeping history & schedule

### Inventory & Supply
- Stock management (in/out)
- Low stock alerts
- Consumption reports
- Inventory simulation
- History logs

### Super Admin
- Platform-wide user management (customers, staff, owners)
- System logs
- AI configuration
- Map services configuration
- Membership packages
- Reports & reviews

## Target Users
- Hotel owners and managers
- Front desk, housekeeping, HR/payroll, inventory staff
- Hotel guests / customers
- Platform super administrators
